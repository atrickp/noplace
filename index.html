Hello! my name is Patrick Marschke. noplace.place is my place to share my work and ideas.

I am a Minneapolis-based drummer, improvisor, sound technologist, and writer trying to make all of those things into one thing. I create custom project-specific sound applications and immersive multisensory experiences that explore the infinite potential of live performance and the uncanny artifacts found at the boundaries of human perception. Recently, some projects have also involved making websites.
