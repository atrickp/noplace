I (patrick marschke) am a Minneapolis-based drummer, improvisor, writer, and sound technologist trying to make all of those things into one thing. My work has revolved around utilizing Max/MSP to build custom project-specific sound applications exploring the boundaries of aural perception through sonic obfuscation and live electroacoustic performance. Recently, some projects have also involved making websites.
