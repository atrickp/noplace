Hello! my name is Patrick Marschke. noplace.place is my place to share my work and ideas.

I am a Minneapolis-based drummer, improvisor, writer, and sound technologist trying to make all of those things into one thing. I create custom project-specific sound applications and immersive multisensory experiences that explore the boundaries of aural perception and the infinite potential of live performance. Recently, some projects have also involved making websites.
